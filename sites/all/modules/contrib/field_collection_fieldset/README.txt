-- SUMMARY --

Provides a fieldset widget and formatter for the Field Collection module.
After enabling the module, go to admin/structure/types/manage/CONTENT_TYPE/fields
and switch the widget to "Fieldset".
Go to admin/structure/types/manage/CONTENT_TYPE/display
and switch the formatter to "Fieldset of field collection items".

You have to define which field is the legend in order for the collapsing to work.

-- FEATURES --
In widget settings you can choose "collapsed" option.
In widget settings you can choose "last item collapsed" option.
In formatter settings you can select the field that will be used as legend.
In formatter settings you can choose "collapsible" option.
In formatter settings you can choose "collapsed" option.
